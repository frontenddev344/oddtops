Return Policy

Return Policy

At Oddtops ,we are committed to providing authentic, high-quality signed football memorabilia. Every item is carefully verified, securely packaged, and shipped with care. Please review our return guidelines before placing your order.

Authenticity Commitment

All items sold through our store are verified and guaranteed to be genuine. Due to the collectible nature of signed memorabilia, returns based on personal preference, change of mind, or buyer’s remorse are not accepted.

Eligible Returns

Returns are only accepted under the following circumstances:

  • The item received does not match the product description

  • The item arrives damaged or defective

  • The item is proven to be inauthentic (subject to verification)

Any issue must be reported within 48 hours of delivery.

Return Requirements

To qualify for a return or refund:

  • The item must remain in its original condition

  • All authentication elements (certificates, holograms, tags) must be intact

  • The product must not be used, worn, modified, framed, or tampered with

Items that do not meet these criteria will not be eligible for return.

Return Process

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order

  2. Provide your order details along with clear images of the issue

  3. Once reviewed, we will share return instructions if approved

Please note: Returns sent without prior authorization will not be accepted.

Refunds

Once the returned item is received and inspected, approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Shipping Costs

  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable

  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the item is damaged, defective, or incorrect

International Orders

For international purchases, customers are responsible for any applicable customs duties, taxes, or import fees. These charges are non-refundable.

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions regarding your order or this policy, please contact our support team - we’re here to help.
