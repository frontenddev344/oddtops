Privacy Policy

At Oddtops ,we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, and safeguarded when you visit our website or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details such as your name, email address, billing and shipping address

  • Payment details (processed securely by third-party providers - we do not store card information)

  • Order and transaction history

  • Messages or inquiries submitted through contact forms or email

  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior (via cookies)

2. How Your Information Is Used

Your information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To process and deliver orders

  • To communicate regarding purchases, inquiries, or support requests

  • To provide customer service

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

This website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website performance. Cookies help enable key features and provide insights into how visitors interact with the site.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with trusted service providers where necessary, including:

  • Payment processing partners

  • Shipping and logistics providers

  • Website hosting and analytics services

These third parties are required to handle your data securely and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

6. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal obligations.

Responsibility for the product transfers to the customer upon successful delivery.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you

  • Request corrections or updates

  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the website.

This website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those external sites.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately upon publication.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the Contact Us page.
