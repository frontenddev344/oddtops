Our Authentication Process

At Oddtops , when investing in signed football memorabilia, trust is just as important as the signature itself. Every item we offer is obtained through controlled signing sessions and undergoes a detailed verification process to guarantee authenticity and traceability.

Authentic Signatures. No Compromises.

Every football collectible we offer is hand-signed and carefully verified. We do not deal in reproductions or imitation items - only genuine memorabilia sourced through trusted signing sessions and verified for authenticity.

How We Verify

Verified by Trusted Authentication Experts

All signed football memorabilia is reviewed and validated by independent authentication specialists. These professionals use proven verification methods, including signature comparison, historical reference checks, and detailed examination techniques to confirm authenticity.

Each verified item includes:

  • ✔ Official Certificate of Authenticity (COA)

  • ✔ Unique verification reference or serial identification

  • ✔ Supporting documentation for provenance and validation

This process ensures every item can be confidently collected, displayed, or preserved with long-term value in mind.
